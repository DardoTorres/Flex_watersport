About us

We are a leading jet ski rental company located on the vibrant Goodman Bay, Nassau. Since its founding in 1978, Flex watersport has provided locals and tourists the opportunity to explore the waters in style and speed. With a fleet of state-of-the-art jet skis, JetFun Rentals ensures an exciting and safe experience. In addition to offering well-maintained equipment, the company provides detailed instruction to ensure that all customers, from beginners to experienced riders, can enjoy an unforgettable adventure on the water. Their commitment to customer service and safety has made them a preferred choice for water sports enthusiasts.
